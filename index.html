Jon Bellion Tribute Page

BIO

Jon Bellion is a Brooklyn based Italian Artist. His style of music is pop based with a strong influence from Kanye West. He is very creative and uses his own voice to record many of the sounds and beats that you hear in the background of his songs. He has also wrote popular songs for artists like Jason Derulo, Eminem, Justin Bieber, Halsey, and many others.
